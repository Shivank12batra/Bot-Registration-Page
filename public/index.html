Attendance Bot Registration

A whatsapp bot which will bi-weekly(Monday and Friday) generate attendance reports giving info as to how many classes one need to attend from the next 10 to reach a certain % of attendance(sub-divided into four: 33%, 50%, 75%, 90%) for each subject.
